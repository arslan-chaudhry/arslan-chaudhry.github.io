I will be joining Google DeepMind as a Research Scientist!

I am a final year DPhil (PhD) student in the Torr Vision Group at the University of Oxford working under the supervision of Prof. Philip H. S. Torr and Dr. Puneet K. Dokania. I am a Rhodes Scholar from Pakistan for 2016. I was funded by the Rhodes Trust for my DPhil. Before joining Oxford, I worked as a software developer at Mentor Graphics for more than three years developing Mentor Embedded Hypervisor. Prior to that, I did my undergraduate in Electrical Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore where I graduated at the top of my class.

The goal of my research is to build agents capable of learning continually over an indefinite period of time. I interned with Facebook AI and Google Research during my PhD.
